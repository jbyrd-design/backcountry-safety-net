Trail Description

Goose Creek Trail offers one of the most accessible and family-friendly hiking experiences in the Lost Creek Wilderness. The trail follows Goose Creek through a beautiful aspen and pine forest, featuring gentle grades and multiple creek crossings on well-constructed log bridges.

Key Features:

  • Mile 0.5: Wilderness boundary - register at kiosk
  • Mile 1.8: Creek crossing with bridge
  • Mile 2.5: Junction with Lost Creek Trail #629
  • Mile 4.2: Meadow area with views of McCurdy Peak
